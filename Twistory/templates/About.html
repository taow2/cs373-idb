Twistory

Twistory allows you to explore famous Twitter handles and hashtags. In addition, we have grouped certain hashtags into "clusters" that can be analyzed to view how real-world events affected Twitter trends.

The development team consists of Nick DeChant, Terrence Maas, Bryan Salas, Young Seo, Wu-Yang Tao and Boomer Truong.
